1. Brand Mission

MySeedBook helps gardeners organize, track, and plan their seeds so every planting season becomes more successful.

The app functions as a digital seed journal and garden planning companion.

Tone of the product:

• calm
• helpful
• educational
• botanical
• organized

Not flashy. Not gamified. Think modern seed library meets gardening notebook.

2. Brand Personality

MySeedBook should feel like:

A knowledgeable gardener friend helping you manage your garden.

Personality traits:

• warm
• encouraging
• organized
• patient
• trustworthy

Avoid:

• corporate tone
• overly playful cartoon tone
• aggressive marketing voice

14. UI Design Principles

The app interface should feel:

• clean
• calm
• readable
• organized

Spacing should feel like a garden layout.

Not crowded.

15. Sound Design

Small interface sounds should feel natural.

Examples

Add seed → soil tap
Success → soft chime
Interaction → leaf rustle

Avoid harsh digital sounds.

17. Brand Rules

Do not change:

• core color palette
• gardener character identity
• botanical tone
• calm UI style

These elements create brand recognition.
