Delete Account and Restore Purchases Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

1. Account Deletion

If you delete your MySeedBook account, your account profile and app data may be permanently removed according to our data retention practices.

2. Subscription Status After Deletion

Deleting your MySeedBook account does not automatically cancel an App Store or Google Play subscription. Subscription cancellation must be completed directly in your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.

3. Restoring Purchases

If you reinstall the app or sign back in with an eligible account, you can use the Restore Purchases action inside MySeedBook to recover active subscription access where supported by Apple or Google Play.

4. Platform Rules

Purchase restoration, billing history, renewals, refunds, and cancellations are governed by Apple App Store and Google Play billing policies.

5. Re-Subscription Access

If account deletion triggers a temporary resubscribe delay in the app, MySeedBook will display the next available date when subscription access can be reactivated.

6. Refund Requests

Refund requests must be submitted through the platform where the purchase was made, such as reportaproblem.apple.com or the Google Play subscription center.

7. Support

If you experience issues restoring a valid purchase, contact support and include the platform used for purchase, the subscription tier, and the approximate purchase date.
