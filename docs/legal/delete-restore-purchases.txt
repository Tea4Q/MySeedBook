MySeedBook Delete and Restore Purchases Policy
Effective date: March 18, 2026

1. Deleting a MySeedBook account does not automatically cancel an App Store or Google Play subscription.
2. Subscription cancellation must be handled directly in Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.
3. Users can restore eligible active purchases from within the app by using the Restore Purchases action.
4. Billing, refunds, renewals, and cancellations are controlled by Apple and Google Play platform rules.
5. If a deleted account is recreated, access restoration may depend on account status, platform validation, and any app resubscribe cooldown rules.
6. Refund requests must be submitted through the purchase platform.
